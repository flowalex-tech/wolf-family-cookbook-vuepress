# Watermelon Quench

## Ingredients:
* 2.5  Cups watermelon chunks
* 2 Cups pomegranate juice
* 1 Cup frozen sliced peaches
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1.5 Cups ice

## Directions:

1. Place all ingredients into a blender
1. Blend until smooth

> Makes 4 servings if using a 72 OZ pitcher
